#=GF CC   transcribed as ~70nt precursors (modelled here) and subsequently processed
#=GF CC   by the Dicer enzyme to give a ~22nt product. In this case the mature
#=GF CC   sequence comes from the 3' arm of the precursor. The extents of the
#=GF CC   hairpin precursors are not generally known and are estimated based on
#=GF CC   hairpin prediction. The products are thought to have regulatory roles
#=GF CC   through complementarity to mRNA. The involvement of Dicer in miRNA
#=GF CC   processing suggests a relationship with the phenomenon of RNA
#=GF CC   interference. miRNAs are numbered based on the sequence of the mature RNA.
