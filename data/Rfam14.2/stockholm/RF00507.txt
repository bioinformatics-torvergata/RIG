#=GF CC   This family represents a conserved stem-loop found in Coronaviruses which
#=GF CC   can promote ribosomal frameshifting. This family interacts with a
#=GF CC   downstream region to form a pseudoknot structure, the region varies
#=GF CC   according to the virus but pseudoknot formation is known to stimulate
#=GF CC   frameshifting. In the classical situation a sequence 32nt downstream of
#=GF CC   the stem is complementary to part of the loop. In other Coronaviruses
#=GF CC   however, there is another stem loop structure around 150nt downstream that
#=GF CC   can interact with this family to form kissing stem loops and stimulate
#=GF CC   frameshifting [1]. Revised in 2020 to expand the seed alignment.
